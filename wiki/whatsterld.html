What's Terld?

At the time of launch, we plan to offer state of the art search technology aimed at helping users find deals to save money. You will be able to search for coupons or for deals that require many coupons. This is great for in store savings and in some cases can even save you money online. In the future, we hope add even more search functionality and other services to make you life easier.

When Will it Go Live?

Right now, we expect that terld will be availible to the general public no later that the end of 2014, but we are aiming to have it ready sooner than that. You can be expecting it in early to mid 2014, but you can try out the beta much sooner if you apply for early access.
