Terld, Privacy, and You.

Here are some of the reasons why we ask for your information.

  • terld uses your email so that we can let you know if you have been granted early access
  • terld uses your first name so that we can address you by name when emailing you. (this also limits the chances that it gets marked as spam)
  • terld does not require that you enter your last name, but it is used for reference and let's us add your entire name to our list of contributors.

we know that your identity is one of your most valuable assets, that's why we are dedicated to never selling or sharing your information. And we guarantee that we will not send you spam.
